# Hi, I'm Vincent 👋🏻
---
I'm a **rising senior Computer Science student** 📚 at the *University of Houston*, an aspiring *Data Scientist* 📊 and *Web Developer* 🖥️. I want to make data beautiful, simple, accessible to all, and a force for positive change in the world.

I started my software journey in middle school writing simple Python scripts — some of which are still up on this GitHub. In high school I competed in the [FIRST Robotics Competition](https://www.firstinspires.org/robotics/frc), where I learned OOP, Java, and how to build software against a deadline. My interest in web development took off at the [Katy Youth Hacks](https://katyhacks.org/) hackathon, where my team built [Food Oasis](https://devpost.com/software/food-oasis-irp89a) to help people living in [food deserts](https://foodispower.org/access-health/food-deserts/). I fell for data science at the [Rice Datathon](https://datathon.rice.edu/), where my team built [Miles to Victory](https://devpost.com/software/miles-to-victory-travel-s-impact-on-mlb-performance?ref_content=my-projects-tab&ref_feature=my_projects), studying travel's impact on MLB performance. When I'm not coding, you can find me riding my bike 🚲, reading 📖, or playing cello 🎻.
